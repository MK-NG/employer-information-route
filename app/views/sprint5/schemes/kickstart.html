Kickstart scheme

Kickstart scheme provides funding to create new jobs for 16 to 24-year-olds on Universal Credit who are at risk of long term unemployment.

Placements are 6 months with 100% of the participants time spent in-work training, to help develop transferable skills aimed at increasing their chances of sustained employment.
