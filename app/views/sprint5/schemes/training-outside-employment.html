Training Outside of Employment

Career advice, free level 3 qualifications, and financial support support for level 4 and 5 qualifications are all available through Government initiatives, without having to involve an employer.

Free qualifications for adults

Free level 3 qualifications are government-funded courses for any adult aged 19 and over, who are looking to achieve their first full level 3 qualification.

A full level 3 qualification is equivalent to an advanced technical certificate, diploma, or A levels.

Without needing to fund the training yourself, they help you:

  • develop the talent pool in your business
  • progress your current employees into higher skilled roles

The National Skills Fund provides more information, including who can add qualifications to the list of level 3 courses being funded as part of the Lifetime Skills Guarantee.

Employer costs

You must pay apprentices the minimum wage.

You may have to contribute 5% of the apprentice’s training and assessment costs – depending on the size of your business and the age of your apprentice.

How to offer free level 3 qualifications

Upskill your workforce by helping your employees find a free level 3 qualification.

Higher Technical Qualifications

Higher Technical Qualifications are level 4 or 5 qualifications (foundation degree or higher national diploma). They align to existing occupational standards, providing learners with entry-level competence and allowing them to enter their chosen profession at a higher level.

There is no work placement element, but these qualifications are flexible to be able to study while working.

Employer costs

None, as the course is not affiliated with the employer.

Benefits of HTQs

Enables the user to pursue a vocaitional skill outside of work.

National Careers Service

The National Careers Service can help you:

  • explore work and skills opportunities for your workforce including those offered as part of the skills recovery package.
  • carry out skills needs analysis for your business to understand gaps and find solutions.
  • find skilled people to fill current vacancies in your organisation.

Employer costs

The National Careers Service is free to use.

Benefits of NCS

NCS can help you:

  • futureproof your business for the future labour market
  • help to develop your current workforce and supplement with talented individuals who have the skills you need
  • increase your resilience and productivity
  • take part in national virtual jobs fairs
