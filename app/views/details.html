Find training and employment schemes for your business

A range of government programmes, are available for employers who are considering hiring employees, offering work experience or upskilling existing staff. Some of which offer financial incentives.

{# APPRENTICESHIPS #}

Apprenticeships

Paid employment for over 16's combining work and study in a specific job allowing you to develop your workforce and business.

Cost

Apprentice minimum wage and 5% training contribution depending on business size

Benefits

You develop a motivated, skilled, and qualified workforce

Time

Minimum of 12 months employment

{# KICKSTART #}

Kickstart Scheme

Support 16 to 24-year-olds on Universal Credit with a work placement and see if they are a good fit for your business.

Cost

No costs

Benefits

Support young people to gain valuable experience and help your teams gain supervisory experience

Time

6-months

{# TRAINEESHIP #}

Traineeships

A 6-week to 12 months unpaid work placement for 16 to 24-year-olds helping you develop a loyal and talented workforce.

Cost

No cost but some employers choose to cover living expenses

Benefits

Helps you increase your capacity and productivity whilst developing a loyal and talented workforce

Time

6-weeks to 12 months

{# T Levels #}

T Levels: Industry placements

Unpaid 9-week work and study placement, for 16 to 19-year-olds giving you the chance to develop the next generation and help them succeed.

Cost

No cost but some employers choose to cover living expenses

Benefits

A learner spends 9 weeks with you at no cost

Time

Short term 45-day industry placement

{# SKILLS BOOTCAMP #}

Skills Bootcamps

Flexible courses for over 19s to fast-track specialist skill development, for existing or new talent for your business.

Cost

No cost unless self-employed

Benefits

Helps future-proof your business in a rapidly changing labour market and recruit staff with the right training and skills from the outset

Time

12 to 16-weeks

{# SWAP #}

Sector-based work academy programme (SWAP)

6-week placement for over 16's designed to help you recruit a workforce with the right skills to sustain and grow your business

Cost

No cost, SWAP placements are entirely government-funded

Benefits

Helps you recruit or train staff through fully-funded pre-employment training

Time

6-weeks

{# SUPORTED INTERN #}

Supported Internships for learners with additional needs

An unpaid 6 to 12-month work placement for 16 to 24-year olds with additional needs supported by a qualified job coach.

Cost

No costs

Benefits

Create and support a diverse workforce and allow you to shape a job role that suits both the needs of your business and the needs of the supported intern

Time

6 to 12-months

{# CARE LEAVERS #}

Care-leavers Covenant

Help 16 to 25-year-olds leave care and become independent in whatever way suits your business.

Cost

No costs

Benefits

Helping care-leavers to move to independent living

Time

Length of time can vary

{# Higher Technical Qualifications #}

Higher Technical Qualifications (HTQs)

Level 4 or 5 qualifications, with no work placement but flexible for employees to study while working.

Cost

No cost but may choose to pay for an employee to undertake a HTQ

Benefits

Developed by employers to upskill existing employees, provide a different recruitment pool to hire new talent and help your company succeed

Time

1 to 2 years

{# TRAINING OUTSIDE EMPLOYMENT #}

Training Outside of Employment

Additional ways to train up existing employees through free qualifications, career advice and financial support.

Cost

Courses are fully funded for eligible adults but employees may need time to study and attend classes

Benefits

Help improve productivity, and develop a loyal and talented workforce in a rapidly changing labour market

Time

Length of time can vary
