Find skills and employment schemes

A range of government programmes, are available for employers who are considering hiring employees, offering work experience or upskilling existing staff. Some of which offer Time1s.

{# APPRENTICESHIPS #}

Apprenticeships

Paid employment combining work and study in a specific job allowing you to develop your workforce and business.

Cost

Apprentice minimum wage and 5% training contribution depending on business size

Benefits

You develop a motivated, skilled, and qualified workforce

Time

Minimum of 12 months employment

Financial incentive

Yes

Read more about apprenticeships

{# KICKSTART #}

Kickstart Scheme

Support 16 to 24-year-olds on Universal Credit with a work placement and see if they are a good fit for your business.

Cost

No costs

Benefits

Support young people to gain valuable experience and help your teams gain supervisory experience

Time

6-months

Financial incentive

Yes

Read more about the Kickstart Scheme

{# TRAINEESHIP #}

Traineeship

A 6-week to 12 months unpaid work placement for 16- to 24-year-olds helping you develop a loyal and talented workforce.

Cost

No cost but some employers choose to cover living expenses

Benefits

Helps you increase your capacity and productivity whilst developing a loyal and talented workforce

Time

6-weeks to 12 months

Financial incentive

Yes

Read more about Traineeships

{# T Levels #}

Industry placements: T Levels

Unpaid 9-week work and study placement giving you the chance to develop the next generation and help them succeed.

Cost

No cost but some employers choose to cover living expenses

Benefits

A learner spends 9 weeks with you at no cost

Time

Short term 45-day industry placement

Financial incentive

Yes

Read more about T Levels

{# SKILLS BOOTCAMP #}

Skills Bootcamps

Flexible courses to fast-track specialist skill development, for existing or new talent for your business.

Cost

No cost unless self-employed

Benefits

Helps future-proof your business in a rapidly changing labour market and recruit staff with the right training and skills from the outset

Time

12 to 16-weeks

Financial incentive

No

Read more about Skills Bootcamps

{# SWAP #}

Sector-based work academy programme (SWAP)

6-week placement designed to help you recruit a workforce with the right skills to sustain and grow your business

Cost

No cost, SWAP placements are entirely government-funded

Benefits

Helps you recruit or train staff through fully-funded pre-employment training

Time

6-weeks

Financial incentive

No

Read more about SWAP

{# SUPORTED INTERN #}

Supported Internships for learners with additional needs

Providing young people with additional needs the skills they need for work through learning in the workplace. A qualified job coach supports interns in the workplace.

Cost

No costs

Benefits

Create and support a diverse workforce and allow you to shape a job role that suits both the needs of your business and the needs of the supported intern

Time

6 to 12-months

Financial incentive

No

Read more about Supported Internships

Benefits

Time

Length of time can vary

Financial incentive

No
